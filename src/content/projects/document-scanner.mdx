A personal product shipped to the Google Play Store — a simple, intuitive document scanner that
lets you capture anything with your camera and share it as a file.

## Features

- **Capture & scan** — document scanning powered by **CameraX**.
- **Modern UI** — built entirely with **Jetpack Compose** for a clean, intuitive experience.
- **Easy sharing** — export and share scanned documents anywhere.

## Engineering

- **MVVM** architecture with **Hilt** dependency injection.
- Reactive state with **Flow** and **Coroutines**.
- A privacy-respecting design — see the [privacy policy](/doscprivacy.html).

A cross-platform mobile app for HyTorc, built with **Kotlin Multiplatform** and
**Compose Multiplatform** — shipping to both Android and iOS from a single codebase with
**95% shared code**.

## Highlights

- **One codebase, two platforms** — UI and business logic shared across Android and iOS via
  Compose Multiplatform.
- **Product catalog** with PDF documentation, torque reference charts, and image carousels.
- **24/7 support** — call and email options plus a geolocation-based specialist locator.
- **Robust foundations** — MVVM + Clean Architecture, offline storage, biometric
  authentication, and type-safe navigation.

## Why it matters

Sharing 95% of the codebase meant feature parity and bug fixes landed on both platforms at once —
dramatically reducing duplicate work while keeping a native feel on each platform.
